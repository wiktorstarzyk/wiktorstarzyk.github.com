Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Dec 5, 2019

Your privacy is very important to us. You own your data and we believe no one should have access to it without your permission.

We do not collect any of your information. All of your data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers.

iCloud

When using Given, you have the option to sync your app data between your devices using iCloud. We do not have access to any of this data. We assume no responsibility for data stored on iCloud.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes are likely to be very minor. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. When we do make changes to the policy, a notice will be placed at the top of this page along with the updated Privacy Policy.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy please feel free to contact us using the information provided on the contact page.
