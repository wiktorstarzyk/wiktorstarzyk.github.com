About Me

Wiktor Starzyk completed a Master of Science in Compute Science degree at the University of Ontario Institute of Technology (UOIT) in 2014 under the supervision of Dr. Faisal Z. Qureshi. His masters thesis was titled: "A Negotiation Protocol with Conditional Offers for Camera Handoffs." He obtained a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree from UOIT in 2011. For his undergrad thesis, Wiktor developed the Virtual Vision Simulator (VVS), an open source program that can be used to conduct camera networks research without having to purchase a number of expensive cameras. Wiktor has co-authored and presented work in the area of computer networks at ICDSC11, AVSS11, CRV12, and ICDSC14. His work has also appeared in the June 2013 issue of the IEEE Journal on Emerging and Selected Topics in Circuits and Systems (JETCAS).

Currently, Wiktor is focusing on iOS and Mac development. He hopes to have some exciting app announcements in the near future.
