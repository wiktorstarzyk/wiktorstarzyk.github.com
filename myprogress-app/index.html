My Progress

Meet My Progress, a straightforward app to help you track what’s going on in your life. Whether it’s counting the days since you started something, marking time until an important date, or keeping a record of how often you’ve done a task—or even pages read in a book—this app does the job. Add its handy widgets to your home screen for an easy way to stay on top of your progress.
